# Working hours for non-EU students in Italy

A non-EU student with a study residence permit may work part-time up to 20 hours per
week, capped at roughly 1,040 hours per calendar year. The employer reports the
contract; no separate work permit is needed for this part-time allowance.

Exceeding the hourly cap can affect your permit status. EU students have no such cap.
Confirm specifics with your university job office and the Questura — figures and
conditions can change.
