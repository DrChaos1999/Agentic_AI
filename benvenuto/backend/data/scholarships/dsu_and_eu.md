# Scholarship paths for international students in Italy

- DSU / regional right-to-study scholarships (e.g. ER-GO in Emilia-Romagna, LAZIODISCO
  in Lazio, DSU Toscana): means-tested grants that can cover fees, meals, and housing.
  Apply through the regional agency, usually early in the academic year.
- University merit scholarships and tuition waivers: check each university's
  international office page.
- Italian Government Scholarships (MAECI) for foreign students.
- EU programmes: Erasmus+ for mobility; Marie Skłodowska-Curie for researchers.

Deadlines change every year. Always confirm eligibility and dates on the official
university or regional DSU website before applying.
